Matt's Sublime Pizza




Description


Prepare to weep at the glory of Napoli with the power of dough, sawse, and cheese (and basil).



Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sawse
  • Cheese
  • Basil

Directions

  1. Flatten dough so that it covers the earth
  2. Apply sawse and cheese
  3. Bake in the forge of Vulcan for 10 min
  4. Remove, apply basil, ascend to heaven
